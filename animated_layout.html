About Me

About five years ago I decided that I was tired of following a certain path of hardships brought on by my own lack of motivation. I realized that I was living a life where I assumed that things would just eventually get better while simultaneously shooting myself in the foot by waiting for some magical motivation to come.

Instead of waiting, I decided to make my own motivation by forcing myself to learn new things, and eventually I landed on programming as the ultimate career choice. I used to want to be a photographer, and a graphic design artist, or anything really that could pay the bills beyond an entry level job. I spent a couple years developing those talents before I realized that I did not enjoy doing them.

But it's different for programming. I can feel it in my blood that I will never stop learning new tricks, techniques, and tactics for making my software development skills stronger.

I have a huge passion for solving challenging problems, and I also love video games, hoodies, and food!

If you want to get in touch with me about potential employment offers or any questions you might have, please contact me at korystennett@gmail.com.

Thank you for taking the time to check out my site!

~~~Kory Stennett
